Manifesto (abridged)

Do more with less

Reduce labor hours. Increase workload. Expect your pepole to make it work. Wonder why mistakes are piling up and stress is high.

Use technology to automate workflows. Happy people and a happy business. What makes you shake your head and say "there's got to be an eaiser way?"

Why5

As a quality engineer, I investigated countless problems. The real issues are usually not at the surface. There is a saying that one must ask "why" at least 5 times to get to the true root cause of a situation. Have you ever seen a hard-fought solution that just seemed to miss the mark? Was the problem poorly defined up front?

My web development philosopy is to look at a problem in its bigger context and work to drive the big picture forward.

ROI is King.

When all you have is a hammer, everything looks like a nail. People that love technlogy often have trouble identifying a solution matches the scope of the problem. Kicking off a project is an investment and it must generate a return for your business.

Waiting for the perefect move means waiting forever. Big problems are really just a series of small problems. Pick something with impact in your business, then take a step forward that makes sense for you. I can help figure out a "right-sized" solution.
